About Us

We are a Midwest-based candle company working with a small team to create a big impact. We started Ivory to enhance beautiful spaces for our customers and to create an even cozier environment for those lazy Sundays. Our mission is to create an ambiance in every space our candles land, to make a house become a home, and to make your environment a peaceful place. We work with our team to create new scents every quarter. Have a suggestion? Send us an email at Ivory@work.com!
